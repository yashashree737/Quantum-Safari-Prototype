The Cave Mystery

How to Play

The Mystery: A hidden quantum bear is trapped inside the cave. You cannot see it directly.

Your Tool:Throw particles to detect the bear’s shape.

• Large particles bounce.

• Small particles slightly reveal edges.

• Quantum highlight the complete outline of Quantum Bear.

Quantum Bear

• Does not have one exact position until measured.

• It exists as a probability wave spread across the cave.

• It's shape appears as measurements accumulate.
